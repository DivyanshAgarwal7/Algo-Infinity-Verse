Treap Dual‑Order Engine

One node set. Two orders. Balanced by a dice roll, not a rulebook.

A treap keeps BST order on keys and max‑heap order on random priorities at the same time. No rotation invariants to memorize, no amortized proofs — just randomness doing the balancing for you, in expectation O(log n) every time.
